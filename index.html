Relax, Explore, Enjoy

Nestled in a beautiful valley in the Swiss Alps, our resort is the perfect escape from the mundane. Dining options include an Italian restaurant and ski-in bars on mountainside terraces. Other amenities consist of an upscale spa, hot tubs, a gym, and a climbing wall. Breakfast is available.

Amenities and Options for Everyone

Escape from everyday life and spend a few warm and relaxing days with us. Enjoy the refreshing mountain air, explore the village, and hike the surrounding mountains. Visit our spa, ski runs, or take a guided hike. We offer something for everyone!

An Escape For Any Time of Year

Whether you prefer a snowy mountain escape or a peaceful summer getaway surrounded by verdant mountains and valleys, we have it all. You can hit the slopes in winter or enjoy hiking, picnics, and swimming in the summer. Escape the ordinary with us!
